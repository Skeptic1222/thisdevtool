What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is the industry-standard placeholder text used by designers, developers, and typesetters worldwide. Its origins trace back to a work by Cicero written in 45 BC, "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (On the Extremes of Good and Evil). A section of this text was scrambled in the 1500s by an unknown typesetter to create the Lorem Ipsum passage we know today. Despite being over 500 years old, Lorem Ipsum remains the default dummy text for the printing and digital publishing industries.

Why Use Lorem Ipsum?

  • Focus on layout — readable English text distracts reviewers from evaluating the visual design. Lorem Ipsum looks like real text but does not invite reading.
  • Natural letter distribution — the Latin words in Lorem Ipsum have letter frequencies and word lengths similar to English, making layouts look realistic.
  • Industry standard — designers, developers, and content teams all recognize Lorem Ipsum, so there is no confusion about whether the text is final.
  • Variable length — you can generate exactly the amount of text you need, from a single word to dozens of paragraphs.

Paragraphs vs. Sentences vs. Words

Choose Paragraphs when you need blocks of text for page layouts, blog post mockups, or content area testing. Each paragraph contains 4-8 sentences with natural variation. Choose Sentences for shorter text elements like product descriptions, card summaries, or sidebar widgets. Choose Words when you need to fill a specific word count, such as testing a character limit or creating a word cloud.

HTML Tag Wrapping

Enable the "HTML tags" option to wrap each paragraph in standard HTML <p> tags. This is especially useful for web developers who need to paste placeholder content directly into HTML templates, CMS rich text editors, or email builders. The output is ready to use without any additional formatting.

The Classic Opening

The "Start with Lorem ipsum..." option ensures the first paragraph or sentence begins with the famous opening: "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit." This is the most widely recognized version and is useful when you want your placeholder text to be immediately identifiable as Lorem Ipsum. Subsequent paragraphs and sentences are generated randomly from the word bank for natural variation. Once you have placeholder text, preview it rendered as Markdown with our Markdown Preview, count words with the Word Counter, or build data tables with the Markdown Table Builder.

Tips for Using Placeholder Text

  • Always replace Lorem Ipsum with real content before publishing — search engines may penalize pages with dummy text
  • Use realistic text lengths that match your expected content to test layout responsiveness accurately
  • For accessibility testing, ensure your design works well with both short and long text blocks
  • Consider using real content samples from similar pages to get more accurate layout previews
